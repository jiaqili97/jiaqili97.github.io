About

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Washington University in St. Louis, advised by Profs. Likai Chen and Todd Kuffner. As of 2023, I will be a visiting scholar in the Department of Statistics at the University of Chicago, advised by Prof. Wei Biao Wu. Prior to the graduate school, I received my B.S. in Mathematics at Soochow University in 2019. My undergrad thesis was advised by Prof. Rui Ding. I was also fortunate to be a research assistant with Profs. Chen Li and Shuang Zhao at UC Irvine in 2018.

My research interests include statistical learning theory, high-dimensional data, time series, change points, as well as their applications in neuroimaging and brain dynamics.

I am on the 2023-2024 academic job market! [CV]
