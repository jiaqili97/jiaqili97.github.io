About

Welcome to my homepage!

I'm a PhD candidate of Statistics at Washington University in St. Louis, advised by Profs. Likai Chen and Todd Kuffner. Previously, I received my Bachelor Degree in Mathematics at Soochow University. Prior to this, I was fortunate to be a research assistant with Profs. Chen Li and Shuang Zhao at UC Irvine. I was also pleased to be supervised by Prof. Rui Ding on my undergraduate thesis.
Research Interests: High-Dimensional Statistical Inference, Time Series, Statistical Learning, Neuroimaging, Optimization

Email: lijiaqi at wustl dot edu
Address: Cupples I Hall, Room 213, Washington University, St. Louis, MO, 63130, USA.
