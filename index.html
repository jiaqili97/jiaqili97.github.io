About

Welcome to my homepage! I'm a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Washington University in St. Louis, advised by Profs. Likai Chen and Todd Kuffner. I am on the 2023-2024 academic job market.

As of 2023, I am a visiting scholar in the Department of Statistics at the University of Chicago, supervised by Prof. Wei Biao Wu.

Prior to the graduate school, I received my B.S. in Mathematics at Soochow University in 2019. My undergrad thesis was advised by Prof. Rui Ding. I was also fortunate to be a research assistant with Profs. Chen Li and Shuang Zhao at UC Irvine in 2018.

Research Interests: time series, change points, statistical learning, neuroimaging, brain connectivity
